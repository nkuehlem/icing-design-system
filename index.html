The Confectionarium

Confectionarium is an online community for bakers and sweet-makers, both professional and enthusiast. It is a member-based platform where users can share recipes and techniques, show off creations, and seek advice. Their image is inviting, social, and delicious.


Icing Design System

Let this system be the icing on your cake! After all the time and effort you put into baking the cake (research & design) let us make sure its best face gets put forward!. Stick with these guides and your project will come out as the next best confactionarium page for our users to enjoy.


What are we?

Classy- We are elegant and simple but not plain or stuffy. The mods will be quick to warn/ban troublemaking users. Vocabulary: No cursing or profane words. Try not to use contractions or acronyms. We may use Elementary and Highschool words. No New Yorker articles here. Extra welcoming to all by showcasing variety of sweets from across the globe.

Social- This means from all walks of life and from any region(good luck localization teams!) Our brand presence should outweigh and specific region colors. No 4th of july red white and blue or christmas green and red. We should focus on interconnection and growth with our users sweet hobbies. Good discussions will be encouraged and shared. Always be open?

Focused- Keep content relevant to baked goods and sweet goods. (sorry if you wanted to talk about your fried turkey secrets) this will help our beginner to our professional level users to navigate to us to find what they are after.

Knowledgeable- Will it work? Does it taste good? Flawless recommendations, tips, and tricks! We will try to have ALL the answers! Let us show our community we are the best by the wisdom we share. Learning all the time. Updated findings will rope in more users.

FUN- But not childish! This will tie our classy and social aspects together. Never scold or talk down to users. Our enthusiastic approach to everything sweet should keep our users coming back for more.

Sweet-(ok I had to add it ) we are here to help everyone be a sweetie pie, ourselves and our users!
